About TGIS

Your trusted partner in innovative technical geographic information services

Technical Geographic Information Services (TGIS) stands as a beacon of excellence in the field of spatial technology and surveying solutions. Since our establishment in 2010, we have been at the forefront of delivering cutting-edge geographic information services that empower businesses, governments, and organizations to make informed decisions based on precise spatial data and analysis.

Our comprehensive suite of services encompasses land surveying, GIS mapping, remote sensing, spatial analysis, and custom software development. We pride ourselves on combining traditional surveying expertise with the latest technological innovations, including drone mapping, LiDAR scanning, and advanced GPS technologies. This unique blend allows us to tackle projects of any scale and complexity while maintaining the highest standards of accuracy and reliability.

What sets TGIS apart is our unwavering commitment to client satisfaction and our ability to adapt to the evolving needs of various industries. Whether you're in construction, agriculture, environmental consulting, urban planning, or resource management, our team of certified professionals brings deep domain expertise and innovative solutions to every project.
