Professional Survey Services

Cutting-edge land surveying, mapping, and geospatial solutions powered by advanced technology and decades of expertise. Precision you can trust, results you can rely on.

Our Survey Services

Comprehensive surveying solutions tailored to meet your specific project requirements with precision and reliability. From cadastral surveys to advanced drone mapping.

Cadastral Survey

Precise property boundary surveys for land ownership verification and legal documentation with millimeter accuracy.

City / Urban Survey

Comprehensive urban planning and infrastructure mapping for smart city development and sustainable growth.

Water Supply Survey

Water resource mapping and distribution network planning for efficient supply systems and management.

DGPS/GNSS Survey

High-precision GPS surveying using advanced satellite technology for millimeter-level accuracy results.

National Highway Survey

Large-scale highway planning, alignment, and construction survey services for transportation infrastructure.

Bridge Survey

Specialized bridge construction surveys including foundation, alignment, and structural monitoring services.

Layout Marketing Survey

Real estate plot division and marketing layout surveys for residential and commercial development projects.

Solar Power Plant Survey

Site assessment and layout planning for solar energy installations and renewable energy projects.

River Survey

Hydrographic surveying for river management, flood control, and waterway navigation systems.

Canal and Dam Survey

Water infrastructure surveys for irrigation systems, dams, and canal construction projects.

Drone Survey

Aerial surveying using unmanned aircraft for rapid data collection and high-resolution mapping services.

Bathymetric Survey

Underwater depth measurement and seabed mapping for marine and aquatic construction projects.

Map Reading Projection & Cartography

Professional map creation, coordinate system conversion, and advanced cartographic services.

Accuracy Testing & Error Adjustment

Quality control and precision validation for survey data and measurement systems accuracy.
