Frequently Asked Questions

Get instant answers to the most common questions about our services and process

What makes TGIS different from other technology providers?

+

TGIS combines cutting-edge technology with deep industry expertise and personalized service. Our multidisciplinary approach integrates GIS, surveying, IT solutions, and consulting services under one roof, enabling us to deliver comprehensive solutions that address complex business challenges holistically.

How long does a typical project take from start to completion?

+

Project timelines vary significantly based on scope and complexity. Simple surveys might take 1-2 weeks, while comprehensive IT implementations can span 3-6 months. We provide detailed project timelines during our initial consultation, including key milestones and deliverable schedules to ensure transparency throughout the process.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

+

Absolutely! We offer comprehensive post-project support including system maintenance, user training, technical assistance, and system updates. Our support packages are tailored to your specific needs, ranging from basic help desk services to full managed IT solutions with 24/7 monitoring and support.

Can you work with our existing systems and infrastructure?

+

Yes, we specialize in system integration and can work with virtually any existing infrastructure. Our team conducts thorough assessments of current systems, identifies integration points, and develops solutions that enhance rather than replace your existing investments wherever possible.

What industries do you primarily serve?

+

We serve diverse industries including construction, real estate, agriculture, government, utilities, environmental services, and transportation. Our cross-industry experience allows us to bring innovative solutions and best practices from one sector to another, often resulting in unique competitive advantages for our clients.

How do you ensure data security and privacy?

+

Data security is paramount in all our operations. We implement industry-standard encryption, maintain SOC 2 compliance, conduct regular security audits, and follow strict data handling protocols. All team members undergo security training, and we can accommodate specific compliance requirements such as HIPAA, GDPR, or industry-specific regulations.
